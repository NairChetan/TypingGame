INSTRUCTIONS

  • Catch as many falling letters as you can within 15 seconds using the pot provided.

  • You have 3 minutes to form as many words as possible from the caught letters.

  • Points are calculated as 2 times the number of letters in the word. Longer words earn more points!

  • Click the "PLAY" button to begin the game and test your word-catching skills!

  • Use the shuffle option wisely to uncover hidden word combinations.
